The story behind our mission to spark joy through thoughtfully crafted playthings.

The Happit Story

Happit began with a simple question: Why does play have an age limit? Our founder, after watching adults light up while testing toys for their children, realized something profound-the magic of play never truly leaves us.

What started as a tiny collection of "grown-up approved" children's toys has blossomed into a thoughtfully curated wonderland that honors the playful spirit in everyone. We believe that joy shouldn't be confined to childhood, but rather celebrated and nurtured throughout life's journey.

Our Play Philosophy

At Happit, we're driven by the belief that playful moments create meaningful connections-to ourselves, to others, and to the wonder-filled world around us. Every product in our collection is thoughtfully selected not just for its ability to entertain, but for its power to inspire joy, creativity, and those magical "aha!" moments that make life brighter.
